Sponsor Myanmar Refugees

Welcome to our page, we are from a small church in Southern Ohio that helps support a group of chilren's homes in Myanmar. The photos below represent the lives of 74 children from Northern Myanmar who are victims of recent clashes between the National military and local groups. These types of clashes happen constantly in this area, and it's innocent children like these who are the most vulnerable.

The team in Myanmar was able to build a new building for this group of kids, and all of them are currently at the home, but they still need sponsors, and that's where you can help! Please contact us if you can make a one time or monthly donation.
