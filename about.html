Who are we?

We are Door of Hope church in Southern Ohio, and a native team in Myanmar who runs a group of Children's Homes. Door of Hope has been working with someone we'll call 'John Smith' in Myanmar for around 10 years, John oversees a number of Children's homes in Myanmar, and he recently built another one for the children displaced in the last wave of violence. Those children are the ones represented on our home page.

How are the funds used?

100% of all donations are sent to Myanmar, we do not take out any percentages, and your contribution is tax deductible. Any donations you make will go to the children's home for whatever expenses they see fit. All of the children will benefit equally, the child you choose to sponsor will not recieve extra food or clothing while their friends get nothing. We see this as the only way to keep it fair. We do love it if you pray especially for the child you are sponsoring, and you are welcome to send gifts or letters to him/her if we can find someone to take the package to Myanmar.

If you have any further questions, or if you want to send a gift, please get in touch, and we'll be happy to help in any way we can!
