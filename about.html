Who are we?

We are Door of Hope church in Southern Ohio, and a native team in Myanmar who runs a group of Children's Homes. Door of Hope has been working with John Smith in Myanmar for around 10 years, John oversees a number of Children's homes in Myanmar, and he recently built another one for the children displaced in the last wave of violence. Those children are the ones represented on our home page.

We don't have a streamlined, professional payment system, for now, we are doing this manually, so if you can help in any way, please contact us on how to send money to Myanmar.
